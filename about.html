About

Mobifest.ca was built for travellers who live for the energy, creativity, and culture of festivals across Canada.

Our mission is simple: help you find the best festivals — and the best places to stay — with clear, reliable information you can actually use.

We know festival travel can be chaotic. Booking the right hotel, finding the best route to a venue, planning around unpredictable weather — it all takes experience. Mobifest brings that experience together in one place, so you can spend less time searching and more time making memories.

What We Do

At Mobifest, we focus on three key areas:

Festival Highlights

We curate Canada’s biggest and most iconic festivals — from world-renowned music weekends and winter carnivals to international fireworks competitions, cultural parades, and food celebrations. Every event listed on Mobifest is handpicked for its quality, energy, and ability to create unforgettable experiences.

Handpicked Hotels

Finding the right place to stay is essential for a great trip. Our Hotels section lists carefully selected properties near festival grounds, chosen for location, quality, and real-world traveller reviews. No endless lists of random options — just hotels that fit your festival plans. .

Travel Guides and Tips

We go beyond listings. Our Guides section covers everything from smart packing tips to hotel booking strategies and how to choose the best festivals for your style. Each guide is written to give you practical, real-world advice without the fluff.

Why We Built Mobifest

Festivals are more than events. They’re places where music, food, art, and people come together to create something bigger than any individual show.

We believe that travel should be tied to experiences — not just destinations. Mobifest.ca was designed to make festival travel easier, better, and more rewarding.

We focus on Canada because it's home to some of the most diverse, creative, and unforgettable festivals in the world — and we think every traveler deserves a chance to experience them properly.

Our Values

  • Real Information: Everything on Mobifest is selected, written, and updated by real people who care about accuracy and quality.

  • Traveler-First Approach: Our recommendations are designed for festival-goers — not advertisers or promoters.

  • Accessibility and Simplicity: We believe finding your next adventure should feel exciting, not complicated.

  • Celebrating Canadian Culture: From small-town parades to massive city-wide festivals, we believe Canada’s festival scene deserves to be explored, celebrated, and supported.
