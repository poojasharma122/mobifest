Calgary Stampede Hotels

Find trusted hotels located near the Calgary Stampede action. These selections offer walkable access to the festival grounds, comfortable stays, and amenities that match the energy of Canada's greatest outdoor celebration.

Fairmont Palliser

Location: Calgary, Alberta

Star Rating: ★★★★★

Price: From $350 per night

A historic icon in downtown Calgary, the Fairmont Palliser has been welcoming guests for over a century. Its location puts you just minutes from the Stampede Grounds, while its classic architecture and refined service provide a luxurious retreat after a full day of festival excitement. Inside, you’ll find polished marble floors, a renowned spa, a heritage ballroom, and a highly rated restaurant. Staying here keeps you close to both the festivities and the heritage of Calgary itself.

Key Hotel Highlights:

  • Historic 1914 landmark with modern amenities
  • 5-minute walk to Calgary Stampede Park
  • Full-service spa, indoor pool, and fine dining

Hotel Arts

Location: Calgary, Alberta

Star Rating: ★★★★

Price: From $280 per night

Hotel Arts brings a refreshing, contemporary vibe to Calgary’s hotel scene. Located near downtown and the Stampede Grounds, it’s known for its stylish rooms, outdoor pool oasis, and curated art displays throughout the property. After the rodeo, guests can unwind with cocktails by the pool or explore one of the hotel's acclaimed restaurants. This is a popular choice for travellers who want a modern, lively atmosphere without being far from the heart of the action.

Key Hotel Highlights:

  • Designer boutique hotel close to Stampede venues
  • Outdoor heated pool with seasonal bar service
  • Two on-site restaurants offering local cuisine

Sheraton Suites Calgary Eau Claire

Location: Calgary, Alberta

Star Rating: ★★★★

Price: From $310 per night

Situated along the Bow River, Sheraton Suites Calgary Eau Claire offers the perfect balance of festival energy and scenic calm. Spacious all-suite accommodations provide extra comfort for groups and families attending the Stampede. Guests are steps from Prince’s Island Park and a pleasant walk from the Stampede Grounds, making it easy to enjoy outdoor activities by day and Stampede excitement by night. With an indoor pool, waterslide, and a vibrant pub downstairs, this hotel covers all the bases for a dynamic Calgary stay.

Key Hotel Highlights:

  • All-suite hotel with large living spaces
  • Close to the riverfront and Stampede Park
  • Indoor pool with waterslide and casual dining options

Quebec Winter Carnival Hotels

Find top hotels that let you enjoy the magic of the Quebec Winter Carnival with comfort, warmth, and easy access to the heart of the festivities. These stays are perfect for experiencing Canada's largest winter celebration.

Fairmont Le Château Frontenac

Location: Quebec City, Quebec

Star Rating: ★★★★★

Price: From $390 per night

Standing proudly above Old Quebec, Fairmont Le Château Frontenac is one of Canada's most iconic hotels — and an unforgettable base during Winter Carnival. With its castle-like silhouette, grand interiors, and prime location within walking distance of Bonhomme’s Ice Palace and the main parade routes, it offers festival-goers the ultimate mix of history, luxury, and access. After a day spent enjoying the snowy events, guests can retreat to the hotel's spa, lounges, or one of several gourmet restaurants.

Key Hotel Highlights:

  • UNESCO World Heritage Site location overlooking St. Lawrence River
  • Direct access to Winter Carnival activities and Old Quebec attractions
  • Full spa, multiple dining venues, and elegant historic décor

Auberge Saint-Antoine

Location: Quebec City, Quebec

Star Rating: ★★★★

Price: From $340 per night

Blending luxury with deep history, Auberge Saint-Antoine is a Relais & Châteaux property located in Quebec’s Old Port district. The hotel is built on archaeological grounds, with carefully preserved artifacts displayed throughout. Its intimate atmosphere and award-winning restaurant make it an excellent choice for guests seeking refined comforts near the heart of Winter Carnival activities. It's within a short, scenic walk to parades, ice sculptures, and Old Quebec’s charming streets.

Key Hotel Highlights:

  • Boutique hotel combining heritage and modern luxury
  • Award-winning restaurant (Chez Muffy) and wine cellar
  • Walking distance to Bonhomme’s Ice Palace and Carnival sites

Hôtel Le Germain Québec

Location: Quebec City, Quebec

Star Rating: ★★★★

Price: From $290 per night

Hôtel Le Germain Québec offers a modern boutique experience in a historic building, located near the Old Port and within easy reach of Winter Carnival festivities. With its warm minimalist design, attentive service, and complimentary breakfast, it provides a welcoming base after snowy adventures. Guests can explore nearby attractions by foot and return to cozy, comfortable rooms perfect for unwinding after a full day of Carnival fun.

Key Hotel Highlights:

  • Contemporary boutique hotel in a historic setting
  • Complimentary breakfast and stylish modern rooms
  • Close to Old Quebec parades, concerts, and events

Booking Tips for Festival Stays

Book early

Rooms near major festivals sell out quickly. Plan ahead when possible.

Stay flexible

If your plans might change, choose a rate with free cancellation.

Prioritize location

Saving a few dollars by staying far away rarely feels worth it when you’re heading home late after a long festival day.

Travel light

Many festivals are walkable — the closer you stay, the easier your whole experience will be.
